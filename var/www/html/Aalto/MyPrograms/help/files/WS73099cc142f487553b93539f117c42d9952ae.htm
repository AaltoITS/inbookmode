Converting Files
 
 
 

The FBX Converter lets you convert 3DS, DXF, OBJ, or DAE (COLLADA) files to the FBX file format. You can also use it to convert FBX files to 3DS, DXF, OBJ or DAE (COLLADA) format, as well as convert FBX files to previous FBX versions.

Supported FBX conversion formats

The FBX Converter 2009 converts files to the following FBX formats:

  • FBX200900
  • FBX200611
  • FBX200608
  • FBX200602_MB75
  • FBX200508_MB70
  • FBX60_MB60
  • FBX53_MB55
