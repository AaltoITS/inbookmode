What’s not supported by COLLADA
 
 
 

The following is a list of elements that DAE (COLLADA) do not support on export or import.

  • Triangles, polylist, and polygons. The FBX Converter exports polygons without holes, and imports them without holes and triangles.
  • Bake & single matrices
  • Variable sampling rates.
  • Absolute Paths (when invalid)
  • Instances
  • Skew
  • Non-standard Materials and maps for different parameters
  • Orthographic cameras
  • Normals (for skin and shape)
  • Light parameters
  • Vertex animation
  • Material parameters animations
  • Effects specialized profiles
  • COLLADA physics
NoteExport or import to DAE (COLLADA) can destroy or produce unpredictable results for some elements.
